Getting Started Guides

What are getting started guides?

We made Getting Started Guides with the purpose to teach people of all ages how to use popular social media platforms and apps comepletley from scratch! We have made several guides and if you click on the sidebars on the side, it will take you the pages where you can view each individual guide. Some even have video tutorials well so enjoy!

Why Are These Guides Important?

A lot of younger people who grew up with the internet might see the guides as unnecessary or rudimentary but as stated, the purpose of our site is to make all information science accesible. With social media platforms being such a big part of the modern world of every day information science, it's neccessary that people of ages know how to use these sites, especially if they want to watch out for their kids and protect their kids' privacy.

What's this all about?

This is a website with materials put together by a team of Stanford undergrads, a Stanford grad student, and some high school students in program called STEM to SHTEM. This was all a collobarative effort with the purpose to educate many about information scienes and how it pops up in the most common ways such as in social media. We as a result also wanted to teach information safety and how to use certain social media sites for those who also need help with them.
